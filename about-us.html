About Us

Get to know us

At MUNLine, we exist to unite a community of future world leaders by providing quality online debates that we guarantee will be online, for free, and forever. We have a staff team of 20 individuals from 14 nations across five continents. In addition, our team’s actions are always guided by our five core principles, namely communication, adaptability, integrity, commitment, and innovation.
